In order to join AWESOMEa group or create your own group at AllPlayers.com, you need to create your account. Registration is free and takes only a few minutes to complete.

From the AllPlayers.com home page, click create new account.


Complete the form

Complete the form, then click the Create new account button below the form.


Complete the profile wizard

After completing the form, you'll be redirected to your new user home page.

A dialog box will pop up on your screen, asking you a few questions to complete your registration.

Proceed through the screens:

  • Verify your profile information.
  • Choose your local time zone. If in doubt, choose the time zone closest to your location with the same rules for daylight saving time.
  • Enter your mobile phone number if you would like to receive notifications on your mobile phone.
  • When complete, click Finish.
×

You can close this dialog box if you would like to complete this information at a later time. The dialog box will pop up each day that you visit to remind you to complete this process.

Verify your account

On your new Home page, you will see a message regarding account verification. To complete account registration and use many site features, you must verify your account.

To begin verification, check your email for a message from AllPlayers.com. If you do not see this email in your inbox, check your spam folder. This message contains a link you must click to verify your account.

When you click that link, the AllPlayers.com website will open in your browser, and your account will be verified. That's it!


×

If you can't locate your account verification email, you may resend the account verification email by clicking the resend the verification email link that displays on your page in the Account Verification message.
